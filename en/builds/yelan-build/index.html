Introduction

Yelan is one of the most popular and powerful characters in Genshin Impact. As a 5-star Hydro Bow user, she brings incredible damage and utility to any team. With her unique mix of HP scaling and damage bonuses, Yelan can be built in various ways to maximize her potential.

This comprehensive guide will walk you through everything you need to know to build the best Yelan in Genshin Impact. We’ll cover weapons, artifacts, team comps, talent levels, and more. Whether you’re a new or veteran Yelan haver, you’ll find tips here to push her damage to the next level.

So let’s dive in and explore the optimal ways to build everyone’s favoriteLiyue spy!

Weapons

Choosing the right weapon is critical for amplifying Yelan’s damage. Here are the best weapons for her:

Aqua Simulacra

The Aqua Simulacra is Yelan’s signature 5-star bow. It provides a massive HP% bonus and buffs her damage significantly. The weapon passive increases her normal attack DMG based on her Max HP. This works incredibly well with Yelan’s kit.

The Aqua Simulacra is Yelan’s best-in-slot option. If you have it, use it on her for maximum damage.

Elegy for the End

This 5-star bow from the Weapon Banner provides Energy Recharge and Elemental Mastery. The passive grants an ATK buff for the team after using an Elemental Burst.

While not as tailored for Yelan as her signature, Elegy is a great alternative for boosting her support capabilities. Use it if you want her Burst up often and to empower teammates.

Favonius Warbow

The Favonius Warbow generates Energy particles on critical hits. This helps counter Yelan’s high energy cost Burst. It also provides increased Energy Recharge as its substat.

For a free-to-play or low spender option, Favonius Warbow is Yelan’s best choice. Keep crit rate up to proc the passive regularly.

Sacrificial Bow

This 4-star bow has a chance to reset the CD of Yelan’s E skill after using it. This allows for more frequent damage from her E and additional energy generation.

Sacrificial Bow competes with Favonius as a F2P choice. Use it if you want to emphasize her E skill over her Burst.

The Stringless

Boosting Yelan’s Burst is key, and that’s exactly what The Stringless does. This 4-star bow has Elemental Mastery as main stat, increasing Vaporize/Electro-charge reactions. The passive directly buffs skill and burst damage.

The Stringless edges out F2P options if you want to maximize Yelan’s Burst nuke potential.

Artifacts

Yelan scales heavily with HP, so HP% main stats are ideal for boosting her damage. Here are the best artifact sets for her:

4-pc Emblem of Severed Fate

This set is tailor made for Yelan. The 2-pc bonus provides 20% Energy Recharge, while the 4-pc set converts Energy Recharge into Elemental Burst DMG. With enough ER substats, this set gives Yelan a huge damage spike.

Emblem of Severed Fate is Yelan’s best-in-slot artifact option. Prioritize HP%, Hydro DMG, Crit, and ER for maximum efficiency.

4-pc Heart of Depth

The Heart of Depth increases Hydro DMG by 15%. With the 4-pc bonus, after using Elemental Skill, Normal Attack DMG is increased by 30% for 15s. This boosts both Yelan’s skill and her normal attacks from her passive.

Heart of Depth is a good alternative to Emblem, providing a mix of skill and normal attack damage bonuses.

2-pc Heart of Depth + 2-pc Tenacity of the Millelith

This hybrid set gives you 15% Hydro DMG and 20% HP. While not as specialized as 4-pc sets, the HP bonus helps scale Yelan’s damage. The 2-pc bonuses are easy to farm as well.

Use this combination if you don’t have a good 4-pc Emblem or Heart of Depth set yet.

2-pc Noblesse Oblige + 2-pc Noblesse Oblige

The Noblesse 2-pc bonus increases Burst DMG by 20%, which is perfect for buffing Yelan’s major nuke. Combined with Heart of Depth, you get a balance of Burst and Skill damage.

Like the Tenacity combo, this set is more flexible and accessible before getting perfect Emblem or Heart pieces.

Talents

Yelan’s talents should be leveled as follows for maximum performance:

Normal Attack: Level 6-8

Elemental Skill: Level 8-10

Elemental Burst: Level 8-10

Prioritize leveling her Skill and Burst to boost Yelan’s hydro application and nuclear damage potential. Level Normal Attack moderately since it synergizes with her A1 passive.

Crown her Burst first for the biggest power spike. Then crown her E skill for amplified damage on a low CD.

Team Compositions

Yelan fits into a variety of team comps to enable big Vaporize and Electro-charged reactions. Here are some top teams for her:

Vaporize DPS

  • Yanfei/Yoimiya/Hu Tao + Yelan + Xingqiu + Bennett

This classic comp focuses on amplifying Yelan’s damage through Vaporize. Her Bow skill applies Hydro for the Pyro DPS to react with. Xingqiu provides more Hydro application and damage reduction. Bennett buffs the team’s ATK.

Overvape

  • Yanfei/Klee + Yelan + Fischl + Bennett/Zhongli

Triggering both Vaporize and Overloaded at the same time results in huge damage. Yelan and Fischl set up constant Electro-charged while the Pyro DPS Vaporizes. Flex the last slot based on need for healing or shields.

Taser Team

  • Sucrose/Heizou + Yelan + Fischl + Beidou

This fast-paced team unleashes Electro-charged mayhem. Yelan provides reliable Hydro application for the Electro duo to react with. The Anemo unit swirls Electro for added AOE damage.

Freeze Team

  • Ayaka/Ganyu + Yelan + Rosaria + Kazuha/Venti

Yelan gives Freeze teams some much needed Hydro application. Her Skill and Burst work great for enabling Cryo damage in AOE. Anemo units group and shred resistances to boost overall damage.

There are many other team variations to explore with Yelan. She’s quite flexible! The keys are to utilize her fast Hydro application and take advantage of Vaporize/Electro-charged reactions.

Constellations

Getting duplicates of Yelan can power up her abilities even more. Here’s what her constellations provide:

C1: Increases Breakthrough Barb damage. Nice boost but not essential.

C2: Buffs Yelan’s burst DMG based on her max HP. Significant spike for her nuke.

C3: Upgrades her E skill to pull enemies together after hitting them. Improved crowd control.

C4: When her burst hits enemies, it reduces hydro RES and DEF for 10s. Amp reaction damage.

C5: Increases her E skill level by 3. Bigger skill multipliers.

C6: Causes Breakthrough Barb to leave a Lingering Lifeline that continually regenerates HP for her team. Makes her a healer.

Of all her constellations, C1 and C2 give the biggest direct damage increases. If spending for cons, aim for C2 to maximize Yelan’s bursting potential. C4 also greatly boosts teamwide reaction damage.

C6 changes Yelan’s role by giving her healing utility. Get it if you want her as a hybrid DPS/healer. Otherwise C2 is a good stopping point.

How to Play Yelan

Once you have Yelan built, here are some tips to master her playstyle:

  • Use her Elemental Skill to mark enemies then swap to trigger Burst mode. This amps her damage significantly.

  • Make sure to use her Burst before swapping Yelan out to get the damage-boosting coordinated attack.

  • Manage her Energy Recharge to keep Burst ready off cooldown. Use an Anemo battery if needed.

  • Abuse Vaporize and Electro-charged reactions by applying Hydro before Pyro/Electro.

  • Use 4-pc Emblem set and Battery supports to spam her impactful Burst.

  • Animation cancel her E skill after firing by dashing to reset sooner.

  • Boost crit rate/dmg to scale her high damage multipliers. Prioritize Hydro goblet.

  • Take advantage of Homtopic Channeling by charging attacks before her E dash.

Mastering these playstyle tips will help you get the most out of Yelan’s versatile kit.

Is Yelan Worth Pulling?

Absolutely! Here’s a quick rundown of why Yelan is so valuable in Genshin Impact:

Powerful Nuke - Her scaling Burst has one of the highest damage multipliers in the game. And it costs only 70 Energy.

Off-Field Hydro Applier - Applying Hydro without being on field is invaluable for reaction teams.

F2P Options Available - She can perform well even without 5-star weapons or cons.

Flexible Playstyle - Yelan fits into many team archetypes like Vaporize, Taser, Freeze, etc.

Future-Proof Value - As a 5-star Hydro character, she will remain relevant for a long time in the meta.

Fun and Satisfying - Zipping around with her Alternate Sprint and nuking with huge Burst numbers is just fun!

Yelan brings so much to any account. Her Damage, utility, flexibility and longevity make her one of the best characters in Genshin Impact.

FAQ

Here are answers to some frequently asked questions about building Yelan:

What main stats should I prioritize on artifacts?

HP% Sands, Hydro DMG Goblet, and Crit Circlet. Get HP% substats along with some ER and ATK%.

Is Yelan good at C0?

Yes! At C0 Yelan is already a top-tier sub-DPS. Constellations just make her even stronger.

Should I pull for her signature weapon?

Aqua Simulacra is BiS but not required. F2P weapons like Favonius and Sacrificial work well too.

Is Yelan better than Xingqiu?

They have different roles. Yelan does more personal damage while Xingqiu has better defensive utility and consistent Hydro application.

What’s the best F2P weapon for Yelan?

Favonius Warbow. The energy regeneration helps her burst uptime and the ATK substat scales her damage.

Should I use an ATK% or HP% sands on Yelan?

HP% Sands. Yelan scales incredibly well with HP so it is optimal to maximize it.

Is Yelan good in freeze teams?

Absolutely. Her fast hydro application off-field works great for enabling freeze reactions.

Does Yelan’s burst snapshot?

No, her burst damage does not snapshot. Only the coordinated attack buff applied on cast will remain.

What talents should I focus on leveling up?

Prioritize her Elemental Burst talent first, then her Skill. Level normal attacks last.

Conclusion

Thanks for reading our comprehensive Yelan build guide! She is one of the best sub DPS options in Genshin Impact. Yelan’s versatility makes her a great addition to any account. Her excellent mix of damage and utility will make her relevant for many updates to come.

Make sure to invest in HP, Crit, and Energy Recharge to maximize her potential. Using reactions like Vaporize and Electro-charged will amplify her damage dramatically. Equip her with Emblem of Severed Fate and team her up with powerful Pyro and Electro units.
