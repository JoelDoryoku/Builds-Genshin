Introduction

Xingqiu is one of the most popular and versatile support characters in Genshin Impact. With his powerful hydro application, damage reduction, and healing capabilities, he can fit into many team comps and enable strong elemental reactions. This comprehensive guide will cover everything you need to know to build the best Xingqiu for your playstyle and team needs.

Optimal Weapons for Xingqiu

Choosing the right weapon for Xingqiu can significantly boost his support capabilities. Here are some of the best weapons for him:

Sacrificial Sword

The Sacrificial Sword is Xingqiu’s best 4-star weapon option. Its effect has a 40-80% chance to end his E skill cooldown and allow him to cast it again. This maximizes his hydro application uptime.

Skyward Blade

This 5-star increases Energy Recharge and deals AoE DMG on normal attacks. The ER boosts Xingqiu’s burst uptime and the proc benefits from his rain swords.

The Alley Flash

The Alley Flash grants a big ATK% boost and increases DMG against enemies affected by hydro. This further improves Xingqiu’s rain sword damage.

Festering Desire

The event-exclusive Festering Desire provides a large Energy Recharge substat. Its skill boosts Elemental Skill DMG, making Xingqiu’s E hit harder.

Favonius Sword

Favonius Sword generates Energy Orbs on crit hits. This helps fund Xingqiu’s expensive Q burst. The secondary stat of Energy Recharge is also useful.

Iron Sting

Iron Sting grants EM, boosting vaporize and electro-charged reaction damage with Xingqiu’s rainswords. The passive also increases DMG against enemies affected by hydro or electro.

Best Artifacts for Xingqiu

When it comes to artifacts, there are two primary set options for Xingqiu:

2-Piece Heart of Depth + 2-Piece Noblesse

This combination maximizes damage by increasing hydro DMG and burst DMG. It offers a good balance of buffing Xingqiu’s talents.

4-Piece Emblem of Severed Fate

The 4-piece Emblem set raises burst DMG and Energy Recharge. With enough ER subs, this set allows for extremely high Q uptime and rain sword damage.

For main stats, prioritize:

  • Sands: Energy Recharge or ATK%
  • Goblet: Hydro DMG
  • Circlet: Crit Rate or DMG

Focus on substats like ATK%, Crit, and Energy Recharge. Some EM is also good for reactions.

Xingqiu’s Best Team Compositions

Thanks to his flexible supporting abilities, Xingqiu fits well into a variety of team comps. Here are some of his most popular team options:

Vaporize DPS Enabler

Paired with pyro DPS units like Hu Tao, Yoimiya, Diluc, Klee, or Yanfei, Xingqiu enables consistent vaporize reactions that amplify their pyro damage.

Freeze Comps

Combined with cryo units like Ayaka, Kaeya, or Ganyu, Xingqiu’s hydro application allows for frozen enemies and the Cryo resonance crit rate bonus.

Electro-Charged Teams

With electros like Raiden, Fischl, or Beidou, Xingqiu can trigger the electro-charged reaction for widespread hydro/electro damage.

Double Hydro Hu Tao

Adding Yelan or Yelan plus Xingqiu gives Hu Tao teams a huge hydro application boost for bigger vaporizes.

Taser Comps

Electrocharged teams with Fischl, Beidou, and an anemo unit like Sucrose or Kazuha can dish out heavy spread damage.

Maximizing Xingqiu’s Damage and Support Output

There are a few key ways to optimize Xingqiu’s capabilities:

  • Level his Elemental Skill and Burst talent as high as possible. These are his primary sources of damage and utility.

  • Build enough Energy Recharge through artifacts so his Q can be used off cooldown. Around 180-200% is a good target.

  • Use Sacrificial Sword if available, or Skyward Blade, for cooldown reduction and energy refund.

  • Activate his burst just before swapping to your on-field DPS to maximize its duration.

  • Vaporize or Electro-charge his Burst rather than leaving it to Tick damage alone.

  • Use 4-Piece Emblem of Severed Fate or Noblesse sets for Burst DMG and energy refund.

Properly building and maximizing Xingqiu’s kit makes him one of the top supports and hydro applicators in the game.

Xingqiu Talents Upgrade Priority Guide

When upgrading Xingqiu’s talents, focus on leveling these skills in the following priority order for the best results:

Level 1: Elemental Burst (Q)

Xingqiu’s Elemental Burst, Guhua Sword: Fatal Rainscreen, is his most important talent. Leveling it increases the rain sword damage and damage reduction. This should be maxed first.

Level 2: Elemental Skill (E)

His Elemental Skill, Guhua Sword: Fatal Rainscreen, generates rain swords and boosts his hydro application. Leveling it reduces the CD and increases damage.

Level 3: Normal Attack (NA)

Xingqiu’s normal attacks can be left at a lower level, as they do minimal damage and are not vital to his kit. Still, leveling them up slightly will provide a minor DPS increase.

Prioritizing his E and Q will maximize Xingqiu’s hydro application, reaction enabling, and damage reduction for your on-field characters. Don’t overlook his talents!

Best F2P Weapon Options for Xingqiu

As a powerful support, Xingqiu can still provide immense value without 5-star weapons. Here are some accessible F2P weapons that work well with him:

Harbinger of Dawn

Harbinger of Dawn is his best 3-star option. Its high crit damage substat and low HP damage buff align perfectly with Xingqiu’s kit.

Festering Desire

This event sword offers Energy Recharge and boosts Elemental Skill damage. It covers two key areas for Xingqiu at once.

Favonius Sword

Favonius Sword generates energy particles on hitting crits. This helps refund the high cost of his Q burst.

The Black Sword

The Battle Pass Black Sword provides healing, ATK, and Normal Attack DMG - all quite useful for Xingqiu.

Iron Sting

Craftable Iron Sting grants EM for stronger vaporize and electro-charged reactions. The passive DMG buff also benefits him.

While not as strong as 5-star options, these F2P weapons make for fantastic alternatives while enhancing Xingqiu’s strengths.

Constellation Upgrade Priority Guide

Xingqiu’s constellations significantly boost his capabilities. Here is the recommended priority order for unlocking his constellations:

C2: Autumn Showers Sweep

Reduces Hydro RES of enemies hit by Xingqiu’s Q. This amplifies his burst damage.

C3: Stream of Exhaustion

Increases the Level of Guhua Sword: Fatal Rainscreen by 3. This directly buffs his Q damage.

C1: Breaking Waves

Adds 3 levels to Guhua Sword: Fatal Rainscreen. Again, directly enhancing his Q.

C4: Embrace of Rain

Extends the duration of Xingqiu’s powerful damage reduction after his burst ends.

C5: Blight of Rain

Adds hydro resistance down and minor CC to his Elemental Skill.

C6: Flow of Homecoming

Resets the CD of his E when Q ends, allowing near 100% hydro application uptime.

Focus on C1-C3 first to amplify his burst damage before the utility constellations.

Answers to Frequently Asked Questions on Xingqiu

Here are answers to some common questions on building and using Xingqiu:

What main stats should I prioritize on Xingqiu’s artifacts?

Focus on Energy Recharge or ATK% sands, Hydro DMG goblet, and Crit Rate or DMG circlet. Some EM from substats is good too.

Is Xingqiu good at C0?

Yes, Xingqiu is still a top-tier support at C0. His later constellations do make him much stronger though.

What talent level is good enough for Xingqiu?

Aim to get his Q to at least Level 8, with Level 9 or 10 being ideal. Level 6+ on his E is also recommended.

Does Xingqiu need a lot of Energy Recharge?

He wants 180-200% ER from artifacts so his burst can be cast off cooldown. Weapons like Sacrificial Sword also help.

Is 4-piece Noblesse good on Xingqiu?

Yes, 4-piece Noblesse boosts his burst’s buffing potential. 2-piece Noblesse + 2-piece Heart of Depth is also strong.

How much HP does Xingqiu need?

Around 20k HP is sufficient. Focus on offensive stats first before building extra HP.

With the right build, Xingqiu can fit into many team compositions and enable strong vaporize and electro-charged reactions!

Conclusion

In summary, building Xingqiu as a hydro enabler requires maximizing his Elemental Burst uptime through Energy Recharge substats and weapons. Sacrificial Sword, Emblem/Noblesse artifacts sets, and crit rate/hydro damage boost his damage output.

Utilizing him in vaporize teams with pyro DPS characters or electrocharge comps enables the full potential of his powerful water swords. Leveling his E and Q talents takes priority, and obtaining key constellations can elevate his capabilities. With the proper setup, Xingqiu remains one of the best support units in Genshin Impact.
