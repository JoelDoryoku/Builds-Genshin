Frequently Asked Questions

Should I triple EM main stats on Kazuha?

Yes, triple EM main stats will maximize Kazuha‚Äôs buffing capability in most situations. Some exceptions are:

  • Running an EM weapon like Freedom Sworn, allowing for an ATK% or Crit Circlet.
  • Playing a hypercarry team comp that doesn‚Äôt react, so you would build Kazuha as a hybrid sub DPS.

But generally, EM/EM/EM is ideal to max out Swirl damage and buff allies optimally.

What talent level priority for Kazuha?

Prioritize leveling Burst first, followed by Skill. Leave normal attack low priority. Aim for:

  • Normal Attack: Lv. 6 max (only if main DPS)
  • Elemental Skill: Lv. 8 minimum
  • Elemental Burst: Lv. 8-10

Getting Burst to Lv. 8+ should be top priority followed by leveling Skill. Some crowns can go on Burst once other talents are sufficiently leveled.

Should I pull Freedom Sworn or go for Constellations?

It depends on your priorities and current weapon. Some considerations:

  • Already have an EM weapon: Constellations will have more impact.
  • Need a good weapon: Freedom Sworn is Kazuha‚Äôs best support option.
  • Want more damage: Constellations buff damage directly.
  • Want more versatility: Freedom Sworn balances stats.

Overall both provide significant boosts so choose based on your needs and long-term goals with Kazuha.

What ER requirements for Burst uptime?

160-200% ER is recommended to comfortably burst off cooldown. Kazuha wants his Burst ready to enable Swirls and buffs. Sources include:

  • ER main stat sands
  • ER weapon like Favonius or Sacrificial Sword
  • ER and flat Energy substats on artifacts
  • Battery supports like Venti, Sucrose, or Raiden

Aim for 180% baseline from subs and weapons. Adjust higher or lower as needed based on team energy.

How important is Kazuha‚Äôs C2?

C2 is a nice boost but not critical. It gives:

  • Damage buff to his midair Plunges in Burst.
  • 200 EM ATK buff for team after using Burst.
  • 10% Crit Rate for 8s after using Burst.

The increased Plunge damage helps his personal DPS, but his buffing is already very strong at C0. The Crit Rate buff also isn‚Äôt that impactful on a full EM build. So while nice to have, C2 is not a game changer.
